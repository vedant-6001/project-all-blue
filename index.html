Project All Blue

Set Sail

"In the world of One Piece, the All Blue is a legendary sea where all global waters and ecosystems unite in perfect harmony. To find it and to protect our own oceans we cannot just be observers; we must be shipwrights. By using technological innovation as our compass, this project seeks to decode the ocean's deepest mysteries and invisible data."

I. Sustains: The Unified Currents

Decode the biological and meteorological data of Earth's ultimate life-support system.

II. Protects: Navigating the Calm Belt

Explore the ocean's role as a planetary buffer and how we can restore the natural shields that keep our climate in balance.

III. Inspires: The Shipwright's Blueprint

Examine how extreme marine environments drive technological innovation and how oceanic stories shape our commitment to the sea.

IV. Your Story: Decoding the Poneglyphs

A personal log on using robotics and engineering to give the ocean a digital voice.
